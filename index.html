Biography


My name is Aliireeza Teymoorian, but you probably already know that. I was born in Tehran, Iran 1985. Gratuated in bachelor of science in Electrical Engineering from Guilan University, 2008 and master of science in Information Technology Engineering from Iran's University of Science and Technology in field of Secure Communications, 2011.

I started my career in Iran's Information Technology Organization, ITO, in 2011, and I'm still working there since as head of software testing department and free/opensource softwares advisor. Also I'm working as a Freelance Web Designer and Developer and linux system level programming teacher as my part time jobs.

Also I really intersted in robotics, and I have many experiences within creation of Intelligence Interactive Behaviors for humanoid robots, and I love science fiction and fantasy stories, astronomy, drawing and writing short stories as my hobbies.
